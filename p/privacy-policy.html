1. Introduction

Welcome to FS Calculator (“the App”). Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we may collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when using this application.

2. Information We Collect

FS Calculator is designed to collect as little personal information as possible. The App may collect:

  • Technical data (non-personal): device type, operating system version, and crash logs that help us fix issues and improve stability.
  • Anonymous usage data: feature usage statistics that help us understand how users interact with the app to improve its design and performance.

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as ID numbers, financial details, or health information unless you explicitly send it to us via email (which we do not recommend).

3. Android Permissions Used

The following permissions are required for the App to function properly:

<uses-permission android:name="android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE" />
<uses-permission android:name="android.permission.INTERNET" />
<uses-permission android:name="android.permission.VIBRATE" />

Permission Details:

  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — Allows the app to check if your device is connected to the internet, ensuring that online features work properly.
  • INTERNET — Required to access online content, check for updates, display ads (if applicable), or send crash/error logs to our server.
  • VIBRATE — Enables vibration feedback for certain user interactions, such as button presses or notifications.
